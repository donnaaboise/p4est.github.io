School

We are organizing a p4est summer school sponsored by the Hausdorff Center for Mathematics at the University of Bonn, Germany. Please see also the school's home page and application forms.

We are planning an online event July 20th–24th, 2020.

Registered participants do not need to do anything.

New participants please apply until July 10th, 2020.

There will be advance tutorials/exercises linked below.

We will keep adding material on this site, possibly:

  • Text and video tutorials, lectures
  • Exercises and tasks for self-study
  • Demo programs and solutions

Those who like to do so will be invited to send in their solutions to the exercises.

During the actual week of the conference, we will be hosting video tutorials/Q&A sessions during German afternoon time and discuss selected solutions to excercises and best practices.
